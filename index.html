Welcome!
I am Jamie, a young artist based in Canada. Here, I share the fruits of juggling my many creative pursuits with the world. I draw traditionally and digitally, paint, knit, crochet, and craft wire-wrapped natural stone pendants. Constantly trying new ways of making, I strive to capture feelings from the fearful to the whimsical in my art.

Your support means the world to me. More than anything, I hope you find something that speaks to you in my portfolio!
